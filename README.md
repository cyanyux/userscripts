# userscripts

Collection of my Tampermonkey userscripts.

## Scripts

### [Coursera Subtitle Toggle (C key)](./coursera-subtitle-toggle/README.md)

Press `C` to toggle subtitles on or off during Coursera video lectures.

### [Hide AI disclaimers](./hide-ai-disclaimers/README.md)

Hide disclaimer containers on ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude.
